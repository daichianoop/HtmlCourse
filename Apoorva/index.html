Apoorva is currently working as a Doctoral Research Fellow in the area of Molecular Virology and Immunology at Molecular Biology Unit, Banaras Hindu University. She has excellent and admirable academic credentials. Her performance in the Bachelor’s and Master’s Examination, as well as her extracurricular activities, fetched her the Ex- President of India “Dr. Shanker Dayal Sharma Gold Medal” at Banaras Hindu University. She has recently received nine gold medals and a prize, including the university's "The Chancellor's Medal," for her performance in the postgraduate examination. She specializes in plant tissue culture, plant physiology, plant ecology, and medicinal plant genomics, particularly secondary metabolism, as a post-graduate student. She relies on her communication, ethics, working on a challenging platform, and grit to gain subject knowledge. Besides, she is an enthusiastic plant parent, a passionate herbarium curator and an amateur painter.
